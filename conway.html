Conway's Game of Life

Rules

  • If a cell is alive and 2 or 3 neighbors are alive, the cell remains alive.
  • If a cell is alive and has more than 3 alive neighbors, it dies of overcrowding.
  • If a cell is alive and has fewer than 2 alive neighbors, it dies of loneliness.
  • If a cell is dead and has exactly 3 neighbors alive it is resurrected.

Controls

  • Left Mouse click turns cells alive
  • Right Mouse click turns cells dead
  • Middle Mouse click toggles cell state
